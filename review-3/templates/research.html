Research Focus

AI Based Diagnosis

Our research primarily focuses on developing AI-driven systems capable of analyzing symptoms to provide early diagnosis of common acute illnesses such as the flu, common cold, headaches, and other non-critical ailments. The research involves training sophisticated AI models using large-scale datasets, which allow the system to accurately predict possible diseases based on user inputs.

The key research questions include:

  • How can AI improve early detection of common illnesses in rural areas?
  • What are the best models for symptom analysis and diagnosis accuracy?

Healthcare Accessibility in Rural Areas

Our research examines the critical healthcare gaps in rural communities and explores how AI can bridge these gaps. By analyzing the accessibility challenges faced in rural healthcare (e.g., long distances to clinics, lack of doctors), we can tailor our AI solutions to these specific needs.

Research objectives:

  • Understanding the current limitations of healthcare delivery in rural settings.
  • Studying the cost-effectiveness of AI-based healthcare solutions compared to traditional methods.
  • Evaluating the impact of AI-assisted diagnostics on healthcare outcomes in rural areas.
